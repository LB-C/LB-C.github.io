The Seats of an Eight

Every seat has a special personality! Read more on the seat you matched with using the Personality Quiz below!

Coxswain

Can't tell left from right but can feel whenever 4's handle height is the tiniest bit too high.

Stoke

Really sweet and a pushover until they are on the water. Then the competitive drive takes over and all the other boats start to fear for their lives.

Seven

Seven seats are the complainers. They are never at fault; never rush the slide, never dunk the blade. That's all 3 seat.

Six

The gentle giant. The supportive teammate who will comfort you after you find out they have a 30 second faster 2k time than you.

Five

Always goes down to get all the oars and helps out the team in every extra way possible.

Four

Always dresses for the wrong weather and asks 6 for their extra layers.

Three

Always rolls up to practice 30 seconds late as to avoid taking oars down. But they'll make up for it by supplying chocolate milk at all the regattas.

Two

Team cheerleader. When they shout, you can hear for miles around. Will always try to pass a high five through the boat (from bow to stroke).

Bow

Always flirts with the coxswain.
